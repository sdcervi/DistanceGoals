About

About this website

This distance tracker is designed to help people who want to track progress toward goals for virtual or real-world distance challenges, if the group hosting the challenge doesn’t have a way to sync with fitness tracking apps, or their own tracking system.

Rather than enter all your distances in a spreadsheet and do some math to make sure they all add up, this tracker allows you to use a simple, intuitive interface.

How is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your own device; you don’t need a login, there’s no uploading to the cloud, and your privacy is protected.

Please note that if you change browsers (Chrome, Firefox, etc.) or move to a different device, you won’t be able to access your data. This is why we have an Import/Export feature allowing you to transfer your data between devices easily with a few clicks.
